## What to look for in society management software

Before comparing apartment management software India options, it helps to know what good looks like in a maintenance billing software or visitor management system for apartments:

- **Transparent pricing**: no surprise charges for SMS packs, payment gateways, or premium modules after you have already onboarded.
- **Real visitor and gate management**: not just a digital register, but OTP verification, pre-approvals, and staff and delivery tracking.
- **Automated billing**: bulk invoice generation, reminders, and online collection, not manual Excel sheets.
- **Audit-ready accounting**: P&L statements, defaulter reports, and expense ledgers your treasurer can hand to an auditor without rework.
- **Mobile-first experience** for residents and committee members alike.
- **Setup that does not take weeks**: most societies do not have a dedicated IT person to manage a long rollout.

## The comparison

### MyGate

MyGate has built itself into a broad ERP-style platform. Security and gate access sit alongside accounting, vendor payments, and resident workflows. It is generally seen as the most all-in-one option, particularly for larger or multi-block societies that want a single system handling everything from billing to gate hardware integration.

- **Pricing:** Custom quotes based on society size; residents may also see an optional premium subscription layer.
- **Visitor management:** A genuine strength, built around authorization workflows, staff tracking, and delivery management.
- **Maintenance billing and accounting:** Automated invoicing, GST-compliant billing, and reconciliation features aimed at audit-readiness.
- **Mobile apps:** Android and iOS.
- **Support:** Support experiences vary by user and region; structured onboarding support is available for societies migrating from another platform.
- **Setup complexity:** More configurable, which means more decisions upfront; better suited to societies willing to invest a bit more time in setup for long-term automation.

### NoBrokerHood

NoBrokerHood leans into community engagement and a broader resident-facing feature set, marketplace listings and communication tools, alongside its core accounting and visitor modules.

- **Pricing:** Quote-based and may vary by society size and requirements.
- **Visitor management:** Solid, covering staff and resident-apartment management alongside visitor logs.
- **Maintenance billing and accounting:** Includes dedicated financial management features alongside billing.
- **Mobile apps:** Android and iOS.
- **Support:** User experiences may differ depending on implementation and support expectations.
- **Setup complexity:** Moderate; positioned as a more complete out-of-the-box experience.

### ADDA

ADDA has been around since 2008 and has built a loyal base, particularly for its accounting clarity. It is often the platform societies mention when they talk about clean books and audit transparency.

- **Pricing:** Quote-based and may vary by society size and requirements.
- **Visitor management:** Comes with a dedicated Gatekeeper module, role-based permissions, appointment management, and alerts.
- **Maintenance billing and accounting:** A frequently cited strength, particularly for AGM reporting.
- **Mobile apps:** Android and iOS.
- **Support:** User experiences may differ depending on implementation and support expectations.
- **Setup complexity:** Moderate, typically demo-led onboarding.

### ApnaComplex

ApnaComplex is one of the older, more established generalist platforms, covering the standard set of society management needs without strongly differentiating in any one area.

- **Pricing:** Quote-based and may vary by society size and requirements.
- **Visitor management:** Functional, covering core visitor and gate workflows.
- **Maintenance billing and accounting:** Covers the basics, including UPI-based payments.
- **Mobile apps:** Android and iOS.
- **Support:** User experiences may differ depending on implementation and support expectations.
- **Setup complexity:** Moderate.

## Questions to ask before selecting a society management platform

Before committing to any housing society management software, it is worth getting clear answers on:

- Is pricing transparent, or will costs grow with add-on modules?
- Are visitor management and maintenance billing included, or sold separately?
- Is the accounting output audit-ready for AGM reporting?
- How long does onboarding actually take, and is support available during setup?
- Is resident support available after go-live, and through what channels?
- Are there additional charges for SMS, payment gateways, or initial setup?

## Frequently asked questions

### Which is the best society management software in India?

Different societies have different requirements when evaluating RWA management software. MyGate, ADDA, NoBrokerHood, ApnaComplex, and Dwell Hub are among the commonly considered options, each with different strengths in pricing, visitor management, and accounting depth.

### How much does society management software cost?

Pricing varies widely. Some vendors use custom, quote-based pricing depending on flat count and modules selected, while others, like Dwell Hub, offer a transparent flat per-flat rate with no separate module or onboarding fees.

### Can small apartment associations use society management software?

Yes. Even 20 to 50 flat communities can benefit from digital billing, visitor management, and complaint tracking. Smaller societies often prioritize fast setup and predictable pricing over deep ERP customization.

### Is visitor management software necessary for small societies?

It can be useful even for smaller communities, particularly for tracking deliveries and staff entries, though the level of customization needed, OTP verification or hardware integration, typically scales with society size.

### How long does it take to set up society management software?

This varies by platform and depends on how much configuration is required. Some platforms support self-serve setup using a flat-inventory upload, while others involve a longer, demo-led onboarding process.
